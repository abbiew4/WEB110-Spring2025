Branding & Design Project


kcpupco


kcpupco is a dog bandana shop I started at 16 years old. I am deeply passionate about dogs and creative expression, kcpupco perfectly blends both. I modeled my logo after my 4 dogs: Brady, Sadie, Kona, and Auggie. Behind them I put the KC skyline to represent the heart I have for my hometown and supporting local, small businesses. My palette is a fun, whimsy lilac color, my favorite color!
